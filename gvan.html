      🏛️ Government Schemes & Subsidies

      India — updated 2025
      🌳

      National Afforestation Programme (NAP)

      ₹8,000–12,000/ha for planting + 3 years maintenance. Apply through State Forest Departments.

      moef.gov.in →
      🚜

      Sub-Mission on Agroforestry (SMAF)

      50% subsidy on planting material, drip irrigation, and intercropping for farmers on agricultural land.

      agricoop.nic.in →
      💰

      CAMPA Fund (Compensatory Afforestation)

      ₹50,000–80,000/ha for restoration of degraded forest land. NGOs and communities eligible.

      campa.gov.in →
      🌾

      PM-KUSUM (Solar for Agri-Forestry)

      60% subsidy for solar irrigation pumps for tree nurseries and agroforestry blocks.

      pmkusum.mnre.gov.in →
      🧪

      Soil Health Card + Free Testing

      Get soil pH, NPK, micro-nutrients analysis at nearest Krishi Vigyan Kendra. Free for farmers.

      soilhealth.dac.gov.in →
      📈

      Indian Carbon Market (ICM)

      Earn ₹400–600 per tonne CO₂. Register via Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE).

      beeindia.gov.in →
      🏞️

      Green India Mission (GIM)

      ₹25,000/ha for ecosystem restoration, biodiversity enhancement, and livelihood generation.

      moef.gov.in/gim →
      🌱

      Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (Agroforestry)

      Financial support for fruit tree plantation on bunds, silvopasture, and bamboo cultivation.

      rkvy.nic.in →
      💧

      Atal Bhujal Yojana (Water conservation)

      Subsidies for farm ponds, check dams, and water harvesting structures near tree plantations.

      atalbhujal.gov.in →
      🌿

      Nagar Van Yojana (Urban Forests)

      ₹10 lakh per city forest for creation of Miyawaki forests in urban areas.

      moef.gov.in →
      🐝

      National Biodiversity Mission

      Funding for native species restoration and pollinator habitat creation.

      nbaindia.org →
      🔥

      Forest Fire Prevention Scheme

      ₹5,000/ha for fire line creation and early detection systems.

      fsi.nic.in →
